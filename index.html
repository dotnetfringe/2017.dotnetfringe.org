.NET FRINGE GEEK TRAIN

The .NET Fringe Geek Train!

Join fellow Fringers on the Amtrak Cascades from Seattle to Portland. Even if you are coming from Vancouver, BC, Bellingham, Mount Vernon, Stanwood, Everett, Edmonds, Tukwila, Tacoma, Olympia, Lacey, Centralia, Kelso, Longview, or Vancouver, Washington we'd love for you to sync up and ride down to Portland for the conference.

The Geek Train is Train #507, departing Seattle's King Street Station @ 2:10pm on July 9th.

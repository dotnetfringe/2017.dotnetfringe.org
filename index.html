Fringe

This what you just must know!

.NET Fringe started in 2015 from a rogue idea focused on .NET developers who break from rules and regularly push boundaries. The .NET platform has leaned up, spread wings, and is moving into new completely open source realms. We're now in a world where Microsoft pushes open source along side the community. In turn the .NET OSS Community has been collaborating and breathing new life into distributed and scalable technology, while pushing a clean story around functional programming and new ideas of cloud computing with machine learning, data analytics, and Internet of Things. The boundaries have been removed and we're ready to keep writing the next page of this story, join us!

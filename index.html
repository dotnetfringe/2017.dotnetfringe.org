CALL FOR PROPOSALS

We are now accepting submissions for presentations. *

We'd love to hear your ideas for fascinating talks and engaging hands-on sessions about
Open Source and .NET. It can be F#, .NET Core Open Source, Best Practices, or how
to control the world's robots with this simple awesome library! It's all possible,
with the core idea of the .NET Open Source Community.

Our favorite topics are those that deliver hard-earned knowledge based on real-world experience and research,
and those that involve audience participation. Come share with us!

* Extended CFP will close end of day (Midnight PST) on May 20th.

Go ahead, suggest a speaker or submit a talk!
